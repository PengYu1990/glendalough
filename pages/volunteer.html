Join Our Efforts to Protect the Environment in Glendalough!

The Glendalough attraction is seeking passionate volunteers who share our commitment to preserving the natural beauty of this stunning destination. As a volunteer, you will have the opportunity to work alongside other like-minded individuals who share your passion for environmental protection. Your role will involve a range of activities, including:

  • Collecting litter and debris from the trails and surrounding areas
  • Educating visitors about responsible waste management and conservation practices
  • Participating in habitat restoration projects, such as tree planting and invasive species removal
  • Assisting with the upkeep of the park facilities and amenities

If you're interested in joining our team of dedicated volunteers, please fill the form blew, we can make a difference in protecting and preserving the environment in Glendalough.
